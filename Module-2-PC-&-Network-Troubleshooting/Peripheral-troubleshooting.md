# 🔹 1. Keyboard & Mouse Issues

### Symptoms
- Unresponsive keys or clicks  
- Lag or delay in response  
- Random disconnects  

### Solutions
```text
- Check USB/Bluetooth connection
- Replace batteries (wireless devices)
- Update/reinstall drivers
- Test on another PC to confirm hardware
